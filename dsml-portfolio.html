SMS Detection with Naive Bayes

I implemented a Naive Bayes classifier to achieve almost 99% test set accuracy on Kaggle's SMS Spam Collection Dataset. The classifier also achieved high precision (98.8%) and recall (92.7%). The features were based on words used, as well as the presence of money symbols and numbers of various lengths.
